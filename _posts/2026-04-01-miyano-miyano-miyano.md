## Appendix A: Why Miyano?

Let me start by saying that I love all you cosplayers. You guys are great. Keep doing what you're doing, and please don't be discouraged by what I'm about to say. I'm just making these observations as an outsider (of sorts) with very minimal experience.

I like to preen, but I'm also a practical person. So these were the rough criteria I had:

- **The character must be someone I know and like.**

  Well duh.

  Now, I don't mean the character has to be from a fandom I'm actively in. I don't consume much of any fandom, for that matter.

  My knowledge of Sasamiya is, of course, more than the average person, but then again, I've only seen the (dubbed) anime. I haven't read any of the manga.

  Does that make me a fake Sasamiya fan? That's for you to decide.

- **The character must be a guy.**

  Personally, I'm not comfortable with crossplaying, and even if I tried to, you'd probably be able to tell I'm obviously a dude trying to cosplay as a girl. Not that there's anything wrong with that (really), but yeah, it's just not my thing.

- **No wigs.**

  Similar reasoning as above, I think it'd be obvious that I was wearing a wig.

- **The costume should be "simple".**

  I already had respect for cosplayers before, but after attending this convention, I have infinitely more respect for them.

  My simple vest and shirt outfit was already enough of a headache as it was. I'm definitely not yet cut out for costumes with more moving parts. This is only something I'd consider if I choose to take cosplaying more seriously.

- **I should look like the character, and I should look good.**

  Why even bother if I didn't?

- **The character should be un-straight (optional).**

  No comment.

With these in mind, my candidate pool was already pretty limited.

Now that I'm writing this, maybe two more characters that could've fit the bill would be [Joker](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Joker_(Persona)) from Persona or [Charlie Spring](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charlie_Spring) from Heartstopper. But nah, I like Miyano.

## Appendix B: Okay, but why Miyano?

> *"If it looks like a duck and quacks like a duck, then it's probably a duck."*

Listen, I am fully aware of the self-image I'm presenting.

I read yaoi, I [write yaoi](https://archiveofourown.org/users/SanguineChameleon), and now I'm cosplaying yaoi.

Perhaps every time I do something like this, I dig a deeper hole for myself.

It doesn't help that the guy I chose canonically denies he's not straight at first, says he reads BL just for the "tasteful dynamics", only to then, throughout the series, fall in love with his (male) senior.

I have no idea what *you* think of me, or what conclusions *you* have reached, and whether or not *you* want to use that as gossip fodder.

But anyway, I'm not confirming or denying anything. Not on April Fools'. Nope.

I'm just here, doing what I like.

And honestly, you should just mind your <strong style="text-decoration: underline">FUCKING</strong> business.
