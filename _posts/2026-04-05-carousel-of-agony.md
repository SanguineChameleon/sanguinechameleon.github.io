I'm a huge fan of [backward compatibility](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Backward_compatibility). Unfortunately, not by choice. If I even slightly increased the size of this body container to ``46rem`` instead of ``45rem``, pretty much all my previous posts would break.

Did I ever tell you I intentionally tweak my wording so it would look nice on *this* site specifically? That's also why I just shrink the font size on mobile so it still looks the same as on my laptop. Every time I see a trailing word sitting alone on its own line, my eyes hurt.

So yeah, I've unknowingly cursed myself. I can really only add to this site instead of making major changes. But thankfully, I haven't completely shot myself in the foot. Yet.

Every time someone mentions AI and/or vibe coding, I piss myself just a little bit. However, [this post]({% post_url 2025-07-27-ai-use %}) is outdated AF, so here's my new, hot take.

Do you like eating? I sure do. But there are many levels to it:

1. Owing a farm and harvesting your own wheat or whatever
2. Buying ingredients and cooking for yourself
3. Following a recipe online but still cooking yourself
4. Having someone help you cook
5. Eating outside and letting someone else do all the cooking
6. Ordering food to your home and eating it
7. Making the delivery guy spoon-feed you like a baby

Now look, I like my food. Would I like to be a chef one day? No thanks.

Do I want to make sure I'm not accidentally ingesting rat poison? Well duh.

So yeah, I'm somewhere between 3 and 4.

AI is smart, but not *that* smart. It doesn't really understand your vision, you know?

They don't call me the *"prompt engineer"* for no reason. 

Okay I'm really gonna pass out now.
